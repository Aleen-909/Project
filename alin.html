What it is F1 is a team sport where drivers compete in Grand Prix races around the world. The drivers compete for the F1 Drivers Championship, while the teams compete for the F1 Constructors' Championship. 

The cars F1 cars are single-seat, open-cockpit, open-wheel race cars with large front and rear wings, and an engine behind the driver. Each team designs and engineers its own car.

The races F1 races are held on purpose-built circuits or closed roads in multiple countries. The race length is the minimum number of laps that exceeds 305 kilometers, with the exception of the Monaco Grand Prix. Races cannot exceed two hours in length, unless a red flag is thrown.
