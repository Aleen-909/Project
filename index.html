Welcome to Gorkha Secondary School

Established in 2051 BS, Gorkha Secondary School is a premier educational institution located in the heart of Tulsipur, Dang. Our school has been dedicated to providing quality education with a blend of academic rigor, character development, and innovative teaching methods.

At Gorkha Secondary School, we believe in fostering a culture of learning that inspires students to achieve their full potential. Our students excel not only in academics but also in various co-curricular activities such as debates, music, art, and sports.

Why Choose Us?

  • Highly qualified and experienced teachers committed to excellence.
  • Modern infrastructure with advanced laboratories and libraries.
  • Specialized programs for science, management, and arts streams.
  • Comprehensive extracurricular activities to nurture holistic development.
  • Strong focus on moral and ethical values alongside academics.

Join us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Gorkha Secondary School invites you to become a part of our growing family and experience a transformative journey of education and personal growth.
